Shipping Policy

At Chatpata Junction, we ensure that your favorite homemade pickles reach you safely and on time.

📦 Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders are not shipped on Sundays or public holidays.

🚚 Shipping Time

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Remote areas may take slightly longer.

📍 Shipping Locations

We currently ship across all major locations in India.

💰 Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your order value and delivery location. Occasionally, free shipping offers may apply.

📦 Packaging

Our pickles are packed securely in leak-proof containers to maintain freshness and quality during transit.

❗ Delayed or Damaged Orders

In case of delayed or damaged delivery, please contact our support team within 24 hours of receiving your order.
