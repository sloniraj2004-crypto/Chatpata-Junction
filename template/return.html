Return Policy

At Chatpata Junction, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Since our products are food items, we follow a strict return policy.

❌ No Returns on Opened Products

Due to hygiene and safety reasons, we do not accept returns on opened or used pickle jars.

📦 Damaged or Wrong Product

If you receive a damaged product or the wrong item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging.

🔄 Replacement Policy

After verification, we will arrange a replacement at no extra cost. Refunds are issued only if replacement is not possible.

💰 Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

📞 Contact Support

For return-related queries, please email us at support@chatpatajunction.com.
